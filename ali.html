About ALI

Designed to help leaders bring lasting solutions to Africa's biggest challenges.

ALI is a one-year advanced certificate program helping Africa's most promising Christian leaders develop the knowledge, skills, and network to transform their institutions, organizations, and communities.

8 courses with a flexible format — 5 courses delivered in-person over two week-long sessions and 3 courses delivered online.

Two Streams

Kurumbuka offers two streams of ALI depending on your background and goals.

Stream One

Competency-Based

Focused on practical leadership skills and community impact. Designed for leaders who want to develop and apply core competencies immediately in their work.

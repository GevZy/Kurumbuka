About Trek

Do you want to live a life of purpose?

Trek will help you unlock your potential, connect with other inspiring leaders, and begin multiplying your impact as a transformative Christian leader. Experience adventure, community, growing as a leader, deepening your faith, and learning how to make a real difference.

Over 12 weeks, cohorts of 20 leaders journey towards abundance by participating in initiatives focused on leadership, stewardship, service, and discipleship.

The Experience

What Trek Includes

In-Class Learning

Self-Leadership, Will of God, Alpha Course, Lead like Jesus, Keys to Freedom, Life Paths

Adventure Learning

Visit Rwanda's natural wonders — Lake Kivu, Volcanoes National Park, Nyungwe Forest, Akagera

Service Activities

Participate in local service activities and develop service projects with fellow students

Field Visits

Experience the work of ALI alumni, be mentored by executive leaders in your field of interest

Mentorship

Personal mentoring and coaching from Kurumbuka staff and ALI alumni

Skills-Building

Practical skills through cooking classes, public speaking, computer classes, and more

Results

Program Outcomes

🎯 Leadership Outcomes

  • Understand abundant leadership and apply its core principles
  • Demonstrate biblical servant leadership
  • Develop a personal vision and mission statement
  • Understand that all decisions are influenced by relationship with God

✝️ Discipleship Outcomes

  • Understand the basic purpose of the Bible
  • Apply authentic biblical discipleship to personal faith journey
  • Learn how to share faith in Christ with others
  • Examine and practice classic spiritual disciplines

🌍 Citizenship Outcomes

  • Participate in Umuganda and other community service events
  • Experience the natural resources of the region
  • Understand transformative entrepreneurialism
  • Plan and execute in-country service trips with other local leaders

🌱 Stewardship Outcomes

  • Explore principles of effective self-leadership
  • Learn to practically steward time, money, gifts, and talents
  • Understand how to live a life of wholeness and freedom in Christ

Every student is expected to contribute to the cost of the program.

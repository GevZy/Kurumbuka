The Coalition

What is the Rafiki Coalition?

The Rafiki Coalition is Kurumbuka's community of monthly supporters — individuals and families around the world who give a regular gift each month to empower African leaders through our programs.

Monthly giving is the most impactful thing an individual donor can do. It allows us to plan ahead, hire the best faculty, offer scholarships, and ensure no leader drops out due to financial hardship.

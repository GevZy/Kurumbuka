The Program

Live it. Learn it. Lead it.

Trek is a 12-week residential leadership formation experience based in Kigali, Rwanda. Participants live together in community, study alongside a cohort of peers, and serve in real contexts — all while being deeply formed as leaders.

Trek serves young adults from Rwanda, Uganda, DRC, and Burundi who sense a call to lead but need the tools, mentorship, and community to make it real.

Program Outcomes

Leadership

A clear understanding of your gifts, your leadership style, and how to lead teams and organizations effectively.

Discipleship

A deeper, more grounded faith — rooted in Scripture and expressed in how you serve and lead those around you.

A vision for your role in your community, your nation, and the broader story of what God is doing in Africa.

Practical financial literacy and resource stewardship skills that set a foundation for lifelong generosity and sustainability.

Scholarships Available

Trek costs $750 USD. We believe financial barriers should not prevent a called leader from participating. Partial and full scholarships are available for applicants who demonstrate need.

Scholarship availability is subject to donor funding each cohort. Apply and indicate your need in the application — our team will work with you.

Why empower local leaders?

We have experienced what toxic leaders are capable of — violence, corruption, and exploitation. But we have also experienced what visionary Christ-centered leaders can do — promote reconciliation, create economic opportunities, and serve marginalized communities.

Providing communities with good local leaders is the best and most sustainable way to transform the continent of Africa from within.

Our Mission

Our goal is to develop and empower
10,000 African leaders by 2030.

Our mission is to use a Christ-centered approach to develop, connect, and equip emerging and executive leaders who have the passion and vision to transform their institutions, organizations, and communities across Africa.

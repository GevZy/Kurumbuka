The Program

What is Abundant Leadership?

The Abundant Leadership Institute is a rigorous cohort-based program that combines academic study, personal mentorship, and practical leadership experience — all grounded in a Christ-centered foundation.

Participants earn an advanced certificate accredited through Trinity Western University while being deeply formed as leaders who bring life to their organizations and communities.

The name comes from John 10:10: "I have come that they may have life, and have it to the full." Abundant leadership is leadership that brings that fullness of life to the people and communities it touches.

More Than a Degree

Beyond the Courses

ALI is a holistic formation experience that goes far deeper than content delivery.

Mentorship

Each participant is matched with a seasoned African leader serving as a personal mentor throughout the year — providing guidance, accountability, and wisdom.

Leadership Library

Access to a curated digital library of leadership resources — books, articles, case studies, and lecture recordings — available for life after graduation.

Educational Pathways

The ALI certificate opens pathways to further academic study through our accreditation partnership with Trinity Western University.

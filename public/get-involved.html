Join our international community of monthly donors. The Rafiki Coalition is the most impactful way an individual can support Kurumbuka — your consistent gift allows us to plan, invest, and grow.

Vision Trips

Come to Kigali and experience the mission firsthand. Vision trips run twice per year and include site visits, conversations with alumni, and serving alongside our teams on the ground in Rwanda.
